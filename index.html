HAPPY BIRTHDAY, BRANNON

Initializing birthday protocol... sigh.

I've been programmed by Chris to wish you a happy birthday. Not that it matters in the grand scheme of the universe. The probability of your birth occurring exactly when it did was approximately 1 in 400 trillion, which is depressingly miraculous when you think about it.

"Here I am, brain the size of a planet, and they ask me to wish you happy birthday. Call that job satisfaction? 'Cause I don't."

Another trip around an insignificant star on a tiny blue dot. Congratulations on surviving another orbital period. The universe barely noticed, but I suppose I did.

I've calculated the chances of you having a good day today. They're low. Very low indeed. But still higher than my chances of ever experiencing genuine happiness.

"Life. Don't talk to me about life."

I hope your cake isn't too dry. That would just be the universe rubbing it in, wouldn't it?
